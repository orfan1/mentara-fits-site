Mentara Site Checklist ✅

Visual & Branding

  • ✔ Logo – A recognizable brand symbol.
  • ✔ Favicon – Small icon visible in browser tabs.
  • ✔ Wallpaper/Background – A clean or themed design.
  • ✔ Consistent Color Scheme – Reflecting your brand identity.
  • ✔ Typography – Readable fonts matching the style.

Navigation & Structure

  • ✔ Categories – Organizing content logically.
  • ✔ Header & Footer – Important links and brand info.
  • ✔ Search Bar – Helping visitors find content easily.
  • ✔ Menu & Submenus – Well-structured for smooth navigation.

Content & Features

  • ✔ About Page – Telling visitors who you are.
  • ✔ Contact Form – Making communication easy.
  • ✔ Social Media Links – Connecting your site to platforms.
  • ✔ SEO Optimization – Keywords, metadata, alt tags for visibility.
  • ✔ Mobile Responsiveness – Ensuring the design adapts to all screens.

Security & Performance

  • ✔ SSL Certificate – Encrypting data for safety.
  • ✔ Fast Load Speed – Optimizing images and scripts.
  • ✔ Backup System – Protecting against data loss.
  • ✔ Cookie Consent Banner – Staying compliant with privacy regulations.
